title: 'Analysis'
---

Get insights into your support channels with Runable by following these actionable steps. By the end, you'll know how to fetch messages, analyze conversations, and automatically share reports — all while keeping your workflow organized and scheduled.

Step 1: Initiate a Runable Session

Start by launching Runable to begin the analysis process.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/f0e22366-7795-4fc4-8af2-a3509fd09709.gif)

Watch this GIF to see exactly how a Runable session begins. It captures the key actions for starting your analysis workflow, from opening the dashboard to initializing your workspace—all in real time.

Step 2: Provide the Analysis Prompt

Type your instruction in the prompt field. For example: "Here is the Discord channel link. Can you get me the messages from last week and save them to an md file?"

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/dbea1edb-b89b-472d-9b15-81f13dd26f60.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/8dae5e60-a73e-46d6-8853-975ee8ccb942.gif)

The GIF for this step demonstrates entering your prompt into Runable. See how the system responds as you type, making the process intuitive and direct.

Step 3: Verify Discord Connection and Begin Execution

Runable checks Discord connectivity to confirm it can access the requested messages before proceeding. Once verified, it drafts a plan and begins executing the retrieval task.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/44282575-669d-48a1-bccb-aea129db750b.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/44f2e9eb-16b3-4c6e-a0ca-987042c65a24.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/a9946124-2412-4936-b3fc-10f2d2ca8373.png)

Step 4: Analyze Fetched Messages

After storing the messages, prompt Runable to perform an analysis on the saved md file.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/ae48ede5-64e2-45de-a22f-21f959f752f3.gif)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/8167bfe9-8a6a-4980-8030-2c9f5077d5e7.png)

Watch the analysis happen live in this GIF as Runable processes and provides insights on your message dataset. You'll see how quickly your request goes from input to actionable results.

Runable processes the data, generates insights, and saves the findings in a txt file for easy review.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/1d02604b-3081-4d4e-b813-f7e147b10a1e.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/7e2ad247-19ff-437e-b99d-e264d3077cdc.png)

Step 5: Share the Analysis Report

Request that Runable emails the analysis. For example, instruct it to send the report to founders@runable.xyz with a custom subject line.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/938b7f40-2b93-4c7d-a337-911d67cce9e0.gif)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/bd7dc4e2-36b0-41b8-9e5b-573b13837509.png)

This GIF shows the entire email sending process as it happens: from composing to clicking send, then confirming the report has been dispatched.

Step 6: Check Gmail Connectivity

Runable verifies its Gmail connection before drafting and sending the email.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/03410fc2-10c5-4d1a-948c-d2eaf9e945a1.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/1ca44323-c05f-4c13-8684-7e1e0d429413.png)

Once connected, it automatically drafts and sends your report to the specified address.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/13acf88a-9d6c-43f2-bf98-447d9bddcc12.png)

Step 7: Confirm Report Delivery

Check your email inbox to see the sent report.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/536338dd-4eb7-48bc-9b47-e2824ce7222a/e6911574-3ad0-4645-9149-ef2a181b2b3b/images/d1db861a-1830-455e-9e6e-63bdc6582d29.png)
