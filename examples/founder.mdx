title: 'Founder'
---

Stay on top of your team's achievements by setting up daily summaries of merged pull requests using Runable. This tutorial walks you through automating PR updates and posting congratulatory messages in Slack, ensuring your team's efforts are recognized every day.

Step 1: Open Runable to begin automating your daily pull request workflow. The animated GIF below visually demonstrates the exact steps to get started—watch the animation to see how to access and prepare the workflow.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/86015ff1-db2a-4c11-8a53-f161db2e2caa.gif)

Step 2: Enter the prompt: "get me the new PRs merged approved yesterday in the main branch in runableHQ repo." The GIF here clearly shows how to input your request and where to look on screen, so be sure to watch the animation for a step-by-step guide.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/36e3d3fe-f104-4c0a-99bb-a5555d9abfe0.gif)

Step 3: Runable checks for a connection to your GitHub account. Ensure your GitHub is linked so PR data can be pulled accurately.

Step 4: The system collects yesterday's merged PRs, with reviewer details, and saves them in a markdown file for tracking. The animated GIF demonstrates this retrieval and saving process in real time—watch it to fully understand how these results are gathered and recorded.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/5f1ea2a8-3118-491b-9378-f68bda50bb46.gif)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/5b18051c-aebd-4f08-b11c-c3e9fbe7333e.png)

Step 5: Have the agent compose a congratulatory message by giving the prompt: "write a small congrats message in the 'all-runable' slack channel mentioning the authors in bold and PR links." The following animation shows this step—watch the GIF for a clear demonstration on how to create an engaging Slack message.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/2ed71ed5-03d8-43e4-a928-188bfc735193.gif)

Step 6: Runable connects to your Slack workspace and sends your crafted message to the designated channel, recognizing contributors and linking PRs. The animated GIF helps you see exactly how the message is sent—view the animation for a detailed look at how contributors are celebrated.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/f19426b9-dc32-4e16-8538-dcd3fa083d0d.gif)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/dac2b856-a16d-4506-b2b6-39022c92ffc8.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/78b8bb28-83d2-4231-b18b-2f15cd83f32b.png)

Step 7: Check the Slack channel to verify your message has posted correctly, so everyone's achievements are visible. The GIF below demonstrates how the message appears in Slack—watching it will help you quickly confirm your results.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/12d27caa-dacc-48e7-8d7b-256960a7dcbf.gif)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/f358a5c0-6a51-4d60-be61-afe354f6d218.png)

Step 8: Save the entire process as a workflow and go to the workflow page for easy future management. The animation here illustrates saving and organizing your automated workflow in Runable—be sure to watch how to finalize this key step.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/49265fd0-3894-41bb-8c9f-027182949bc9.gif)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/782a7b95-9990-4d2e-ba9e-7b1162a66bf8.png)

Step 9: Specify the exact Slack channel for notifications, so your messages always reach the right audience.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/cd3d2910-da21-4d78-9ca1-2d9f356c5d60.png)![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/fee81f36-ce5a-42af-9ffd-a67e62e488e3.png)

Step 10: Schedule your workflow to run every day at 6pm. The animated GIF below demonstrates how to set this schedule—watch closely to ensure daily, timely updates for your team.![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/c3cb82fd-0365-4800-9910-5cac56195fbc/5639abb2-df44-4f97-9ce0-8126ccdc3dec/images/810d2807-9a77-48bf-acac-958def6736ee.gif)

You’ve now automated your team's daily PR celebrations—helping keep everyone engaged and recognized for their hard work!
