Automate Your Sales Meeting Research with Runable

Stay prepared for sales meetings with this guide on using Runable to automatically collect meeting details, research companies and contacts, and update your notes—all before you start your day.

Step 1: Start with Your Prompt Kick things off by creating a prompt in Runable: "Check my calendar today and see if I have a meeting with any company. If yes, save the details in today meetings.md file with all the details like meeting attendees, emails, some IDs, etc.—everything important."
