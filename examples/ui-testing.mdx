title: 'UI Testing'
---

Automate and monitor your UI testing with Runable by setting up a workflow that checks connector functionality and sends status updates to your Slack channel. You'll use animated GIFs to see exactly how to perform each key action, making the setup process clear and engaging.

**Step 1:** Open Runable and access the Connectors page. In the search bar, enter "gmail" to locate the Gmail connector.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/12957a5f-4257-4b83-8e5c-78878dd48996/d43837d0-9a95-499d-80c3-04725c1005e6/images/342d0246-ce1e-47e3-abc4-03e722323038.gif)\*This GIF shows how to navigate to the Connectors page and search for "gmail," demonstrating the exact interface interactions to find the connector.\*

**Step 2:** Verify that Gmail appears in the search results. Prompt Runable to check whether Gmail is available, then instruct it to share the status in your Slack channel **#ui-testing**.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/12957a5f-4257-4b83-8e5c-78878dd48996/d43837d0-9a95-499d-80c3-04725c1005e6/images/0cb313dc-733a-4347-a1b8-f79f666cfc65.gif)\*This GIF illustrates the process of verifying the Gmail connector's presence and status, and initiating the command to share results to your designated Slack channel.\*

**Step 3:** If Slack integration isn't connected yet, authorize Runable to connect with your Slack account. Click **Continue** to proceed through the authentication.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/12957a5f-4257-4b83-8e5c-78878dd48996/d43837d0-9a95-499d-80c3-04725c1005e6/images/5ab39994-006a-47fc-9abb-8ec7dd3c5161.png)

**Step 4:** Once connected, confirm that Runable has sent the UI test status directly to your Slack channel.

![](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/138e6e12-51a4-4458-9914-1bc6359a8384/12957a5f-4257-4b83-8e5c-78878dd48996/d43837d0-9a95-499d-80c3-04725c1005e6/images/d36d7c29-fad6-49a5-8274-1b5b64ea765b.gif)\*This GIF demonstrates confirming that the Slack integration works by showing the real-time delivery of a UI test status notification in your channel.\*

**Step 5:** Save your testing workflow. Go to the workflow page to review or modify the setup.
