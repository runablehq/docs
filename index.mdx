## How It Works

1. **Human approach** – Ask Runable to do something. It will plan the task and begin working.
2. **Human feedback** – If anything is needed, Runable will ask you to provide it.
3. **Repeat** – Repeat the above steps to request follow-up tasks.
4. **Done** – Save the chat to convert it into a workflow.
5. **Schedule** – Schedule tasks by telling Runable when to run, in plain English.

Overall goal : Test if connectors are loading and verify that the “Gmail” connector is available on [runable.com](http://runable.com) every day at 10 AM, then share the status on the Slack channel.
