---
title: "How to Authenticate Connector?"
description: "Guide to connect with connectors like Gmail, Notion, Calendar, Airtable, Google docs, Google sheets etc."
---

There are 2 ways in which you can connect with a connector.

## Before starting a new chat

1. Click Connectors.
2. Search for the connector you want to connect.
3. Click Connect.
4. Follow the steps in the pop-up.
5. You’ll see a confirmation once the connector is successfully connected.

## During a chat

1. The agent will automatically prompt you to connect the connector.
2. Click Connect.
3. Follow the steps in the pop-up.
4. You’ll see a confirmation once the connector is successfully connected.
