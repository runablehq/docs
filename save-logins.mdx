---
title: 'How to Save Login To Runable Computer?'
description: 'Guide to save logins permanently to Runable's Computer.'
---

## Step 1 - Visit website

Navigate to the homepage and enter “go to gmail.com” (or any other site you want to log in to).

## Step 2 - Manually Submit Credentials

Take control of runable's cloud computer and log in using your credentials. We never store your credentials—only an encrypted token.

## Step 3 - Securely Save Credentials

Click the Save button to store that token permanently in the Runable system.

## Step 4 - New Chat 

Now When you start any new chat, you’ll already be logged in to that website.
